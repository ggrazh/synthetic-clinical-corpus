John's diagnosis of arterial hypertension was confirmed based on the average of 2 blood pressure measurements taken at different times under specific conditions. Home or ambulatory BP monitoring was not indicated in this case.

John was started on an angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE) inhibitor, lisinopril 20 mg once daily, in addition to his existing medications. His medication regimen was adjusted to include:
